OUR COMMUNITIES:
Impact Business Community,
with nearly 100 Ukrainian impact entrepreneurs, fostering a network of support and collaboration.,
Community of Ukrainian Female Entrepreneurs,
with nearly 700 female entrepreneurs, serving as a powerful platform for Ukrainian women business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs to connect, share resources, and foster collaboration in the women’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.
Sustainable Energy Network,
a community of innovative stakeholders and forward-thinking leaders in the energy sector in Ukraine to establish an inclusive ecosystem that empowers energy-efficient reconstruction efforts and market transformation.
Network of Women Veterans,
a community for female Ukrainian veterans and their families, fostering a network of support and empowerment.
