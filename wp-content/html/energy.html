ABOUT

The Programme is aimed at cultivating a new generation of forward thinking leaders and establishing an inclusive ecosystem that empowers energy innovators to drive energy-efficient reconstruction efforts and propel Ukraine towards a resilient and sustainable energy future.

The initiative seeks to map the market players in the field of energy efficiency and equip them with the skills and expertise necessary to implement a meaningful change in rebuilding the energy sector and economy of Ukraine.

Testimonials

Programs like "Innovators of Energy Efficiency of Ukraine" play an extremely important role in modern realities.
I am convinced that they provide participants not only with a unique opportunity to receive valuable consultations from leading experts but also help establish a network of contacts and understand the principles of the energy efficiency sector. Such programs help improve business plans and presentations, making participants' projects more commercially attractive.

Demo Day was the culmination of the program, where 15 finalists had the opportunity to present their innovative solutions to experts and potential investors. I was pleasantly surprised by the level of project implementation and the creative approach to energy efficiency issues.

I am confident that this program will open up real opportunities for participants to develop their businesses both in the domestic and international markets. The innovative solutions presented on Demo Day only emphasize that Ukraine has every chance to become one of the leaders in energy-efficient technologies and solutions. This indicates the significant potential of the country in this promising field and contributes to its integration into the global energy-efficient community.

Criteria

Legal entity and activities of the Organization.
The Organization is registered and operates in Ukraine for at least one year. The Organization has at least three (3) full-time employees.
Business activity and plans
The Organization demonstrates a clear business plan and a potential for development.
Innovations in energy efficiency
The Organization has a component of an innovative idea or solution with regard to energy efficiency that contributes to or can potentially be useful for the energy sector of Ukraine.

Initiators

The "Innovators in Energy Efficiency for Ukraine" program was initiated by Impact Force with the support of the "Promotion of Energy Efficiency and Implementation of the EU Directive on Energy Efficiency in Ukraine" project, implemented by GIZ on behalf of the German government and co-financed by the Swiss government, in cooperation with the Ministry of Digital Transformation of Ukraine, the Office of Entrepreneurship and Export Promotion, Diia.Business as well as the State Agency for Energy Efficiency and Energy Saving of Ukraine.

The program is aimed at shaping a new generation of forward- thinking leaders and creating an inclusive ecosystem that will enable energy innovators to move Ukraine towards a resilient and sustainable future.
